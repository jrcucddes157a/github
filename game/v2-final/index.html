裁判の決闘

How to Play

Basics

Battle Court (裁判の決闘) is a two player battle RPG game in which you and another player compete to see who can drain their opponent's HP to 0 first.

You have 3 different actions at your disposal: Attack, Healing, and Using a Card. Using any of these actions uses a turn and the next player goes.

Turn Indication

When it is your turn, your player number above your character will turn gold.

Rolling Dice

In order to Attack or Heal, you must roll the dice to determine how much hit points (HP) you can either deplete from your opponent, or heal yourself with.

You can roll up to three times, with the third time entering Overburst Mode.

In Overburst Mode, your third roll has the potential to add the most damage to heal or attack with, but with a risky cost. If your rolls are not a 6 or higher, you will fail the Overburst and forfeit your turn, making you unable to attack, heal, or use a card.

Healing

To heal, you use the amount you roll the dice with. You have a maximum of three heals for the entire game. Be sure to use them wisely.

Cards

Cards are a literal "trump card" that can give you advantage in battle. You can use them at any time once. Your card is given to you face down and can be any of the following with the effects:

  • JOKER
    Swaps the HP values of yourself
    and your opponent immediately.

  • JACK
    Doubles the value of your first
    roll on the next turn.

  • QUEEN
    Halves your opponent's attack on the next turn.

  • KING
    Lets you roll 4 dice for all
    3 rolls on the next turn.

  • GOD
    Deals an instant, immediate attack
    worth 25% of your current HP.

Once used, your card will remain as long until the effect is done. After that, it will disappear.

You will get a new card only after you have had 5 turns of your plays pass.
